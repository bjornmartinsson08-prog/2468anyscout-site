Introducing AnyScout

A robust data collection and analysis system tailored for all FRC teams, by FRC Team 2468.

What is AnyScout?

AnyScout is a scouting system developed by FRC team 2468 to make the enriching and educational experience of data analysis accessible to all FRC teams. AnyScout provides teams with the ability to make information based decisions during alliance selection and develop match based strategies through a data collection and analysis solution. Our software consists of an input form that allows for comprehensive data capture across any device. For in depth-analysis, AnyScout offers pre-made calculations, graphs, and tables that create and display important info pertaining to teams and competitions. Most of all, AnyScout is customizable, providing ample room for creativity and variation in your scouting app.

Why did we make AnyScout?

Our hope with AnyScout is continuing to help teams break down barriers preventing them from participating in data analysis within an FRC setting. In our system, there is less hassle to set up an app, greater ease of access, and more room to innovate. By encouraging interest in scouting and data analysis, we strive to open eyes to a whole new world of FRC, allowing for deep engagement and strategy within matches.

Our aim is to unlock every team's full competitive potential by increasing access to data-based decision making. Join us by making your app here, and elevate your team's success on the playing field, and appreciate increased competition and enjoyment of FRC.

Returning Users

We value teaching data analysis skills and supporting each team's individuality in creating a scouting app to fit their specific needs. So, this year, we prioritized providing a solid foundation for teams to build on, allowing for ample room for our app to grow to fit each team's mold. We hope to encourage creativity while still providing software that can successfully provide data to any team.
